Makassar

About Makassar

Makassar, the provincial capital of South Sulawesi, Indonesia, stands as a beacon of history and modernity on the Sulawesi coast. As the largest city in eastern Indonesia, Makassar has played a pivotal role in regional trade and cultural exchanges for centuries...

Geography of Makassar

Makassar is located on the southwest coast of the island of Sulawesi, overlooking the Makassar Strait. The city is situated near the equator, resulting in a tropical climate...

Geographical Facts:

  • Coordinates: 5.1476° S, 119.4327° E
  • Climate: Tropical, with wet and dry seasons
  • Area: 199.3 km²
  • Population: Approximately 1.4 million
  • Key Features: Losari Beach, Trans Studio Makassar, and Port of Makassar
